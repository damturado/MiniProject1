FAQs

Boarding Requirements

Any valid government-issued IDs such as driver's license, Pag-ibig, UMID, TIN, Philhealth, Postal ID Card, police clearance and most importantly vaccination ID.

Boarding Cut-Off Time

Travelers must be at the terminal port at least an hour to process boarding pass from our shipping partners to confirm their seats before check-in.

Payment Method

Here in ByaheTa! We offers convenient payment arrangement through GCash, GCredit, credit/debit card through BPI and Union Bank.

ETicket
